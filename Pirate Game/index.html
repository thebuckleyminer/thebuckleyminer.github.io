The Legend of Pirate Island

Link to Project Documentation Page

Yer in need of a Crew and have come to the right place!

In this Crew Building four player game each player has a different role! Split into two teams, each team has one Captain who will oversee the operation, and a First Mate who will do the heavy lifting of getting new recuits.

The board consists of 25 cards. Each card has a Pirate nickname on it and it is your team's objective to collect your whole crew first.

Each team has eight or nine recruit cards that correspond to their crew's color.

There is one Black card that that is chosen is an instant game over, and the remaining are neutral.

The Captain has the ability to see the bottom colored side of the cards and must give the First Mate a one or two word clue for what pirates to recruit.

The First Mate can only see the Tan side of the recuit cards and must make a guess to what the Captain was thinking.


This game is similar to a reverse battleship senario where you are trying to blindly guide your team to the card you want them to!


-This game is heavily based off of Codenames. I do not intend to make this public outside the scope of this class, nor do I strive to make any money off of it.
