The Legend of Pirate Island

If yer in need of a Crew ye have come to the right place!

In this Crew Building four player game each player has a different role! Split into two teams, each team has one Captain who will oversee the operation, and a First Mate who will do the heavy lifting of getting new recruits.

The board consists of 25 cards. Each card has a Pirate nickname on it and it is your team's objective to collect your whole crew first.

Each team has eight or nine recruit cards that correspond to their crew's color.

There is one Black card that that is chosen is an instant game over, and the remaining are neutral.

The Captain has the ability to see the bottom colored side of the cards and must give the First Mate a one or two word clue for what pirates to recruit.

The First Mate can only see the Tan side of the recruit cards and must make a guess to what the Captain was thinking.

This game is like Battleship but in reverse! The Captain knows where he wants the First Mate to look and must provide clues of where to look!

**This game is heavily based off of the classic board game: "Codenames". Links to the official codenames site are located below.
